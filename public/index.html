Questions

Good to know

Is it safe?

Yes. Tracking is fully read-only: paste an address and MonScope reads public chain data, no wallet connection. The only action that ever touches your wallet is optional revoking, which sends a single transaction to set a token allowance to zero. It can never move your funds.

What does it track?

Token holdings, DeFi positions, open trades, NFTs, unclaimed rewards, recent activity and your total gas spent, all read live from Monad chain 143.

Where do prices come from?

Anchors from CoinGecko and the long tail from Monad DEX liquidity. Anything we cannot price reliably is quarantined and never counted toward your net worth, so the number stays honest.

Do I need an account?

No signup, no wallet connection, no token. Just paste any Monad address, or share one to look someone up.

How current is it?

Balances and positions are read live on every load, not from a stale cache. Monad finalises in under a second, so what you see is essentially now.

What does it cost?

Free while we are in early access.
